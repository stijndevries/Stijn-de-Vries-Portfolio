Eudoxus Sans

Eudoxus Sans is a modified version of +Jakarta Sans. It has simple geometric shapes with rounded accents. It's a work in progress and will keep changing in the coming months. The font is perfect for headlines, logos, badges or any other design project.

The name is based on the Greek mathematician Eudoxus of Cnidus who was one of the first to develop mathematic theories on proportions and geometry.

Eudoxus Sans is free and released under the Open Font License.

You can download and use Eudoxus Sans for personal and commercial projects or you can easily embed the fonts by including these links in the Header of your website:
